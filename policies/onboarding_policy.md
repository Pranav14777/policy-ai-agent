# Onboarding Guide for New Employees

1. On your first day, report to Reception at 09:00 for badge activation and workspace assignment.
2. You will receive your laptop and initial credentials from IT during the welcome session.
3. Within the first week, complete the mandatory e-learning modules on security, safety, and company culture.
4. Your manager will schedule a team introduction and a 1:1 onboarding meeting in the first three working days.
5. Every new employee is assigned a buddy who can answer practical questions during the first three months.
6. Probation period evaluations take place after 3 months and must be documented in the HR system.
