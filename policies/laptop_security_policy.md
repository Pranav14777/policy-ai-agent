# Laptop Usage & Security Policy

1. Company laptops are for business use. Limited personal use is allowed if it does not conflict with work.
2. Devices must be locked (Windows key + L) whenever left unattended.
3. Full disk encryption and company antivirus software must remain enabled at all times.
4. Employees must never share their account password or login token with anyone, including colleagues or IT.
5. Storing confidential data on personal cloud services (e.g. personal Google Drive, Dropbox) is prohibited.
6. Lost or stolen devices must be reported to IT Security within 1 hour after discovery.
7. Only approved VPN software may be used to access internal systems from outside the office.
