# Travel & Expense Policy

1. Employees must book travel through the company-approved booking portal whenever possible.
2. Economy class is the default for flights shorter than 6 hours. Business class requires manager approval.
3. All hotel bookings should be within the allowed nightly budget specified by your department.
4. Receipts must be uploaded within 5 working days after the trip using the Expense Portal.
5. Taxi or ride-sharing costs are reimbursed only when public transport is not practical or safe.
6. Per diem covers meals and small expenses; separate receipts for meals are not required if per diem is used.
