# Remote Work Policy

1. Employees may work remotely up to 3 days per week unless team-specific arrangements differ.
2. Working from a public place (cafes, airports) requires using the company VPN at all times.
3. Confidential meetings must not be held in public environments where conversations can be overheard.
4. Employees must ensure stable internet, appropriate workspace, and access to all required systems.
5. Any equipment taken home (monitors, keyboards, docking stations) must be registered with IT.
6. Working outside the country requires manager approval and must follow tax and legal guidelines.
